STEP 1:
SAMPLING

In music, sampling is the act of taking a portion, or sample, of one sound recording and reusing it as an instrument or a sound recording in a different song or piece.

We record sounds and we manipulate our samples to your wishes. While we are recording and manipulating the sounds, you are able to sit right next to us and give us feedback at the moment.

After the process is done. You are able to chose which format the samples you want or we go to the next step: mastering the samples.

STEP 2:
RECORDING

We record using Digital Recording. We use the best techniques to get what you want.

We record your sounds and voices with the 'POWER DYNAMICS PDS-M01 STUDIO CONDENSATOR' and we use the best software called Ableton Live 9. Our record studio is special made for voice records. It has optimized lengths and special material to record the voices as natural as possible.

After recording, we go through the sampling process.

STEP 3:
MASTERING

After going threw the previous steps, your sound is almost ready to be published and shared to the whole world. But first, your sound need to be mastered.

We make your music sound perfect. We provide rofessional mastering to labels, producers, artists and DJs. Raising your track to a higher level is our passion. Thanks to our mastering skills, your track will be balanced to perfection. The best analogue equipment and acoustics make your music sound good everywhere. Whether it's loud, bright and sparkling masters; Or to a warmer sound with more dynamics. We use different techniques for what you want: vinyl mastering or CD mastering...Figround Projects puts it down for you.

After the previous steps and the mastering process is your ready to be shared to the world!
